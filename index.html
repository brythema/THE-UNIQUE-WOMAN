✧ Our Story

Not an organisation.
A movement of women.

The Unique Woman began as a quiet conviction in one woman's heart — that when God transforms a woman, He transforms the home, the church and the nation she touches.

Our History

What started as prayer meetings among a handful of women in Abuja grew, by God's grace, into a movement reaching students, mothers, missionaries and pastors' wives across Nigeria. Every chapter — an outreach, a scholarship, a retreat, a devotional written at midnight — has been an act of obedience before it was ever a program.

Today, The Unique Woman exists to disciple women into becoming spiritually mature, biblically grounded, emotionally healthy, intellectually equipped and practically useful in God's Kingdom — one life, one household, one generation at a time.

📖

Meet the Founder

A daughter, a mother, a minister — carrying a burden for women after God's own heart.

Founder · The Unique Woman

Enayi J. Ameh

Enayi J. Ameh is a wife, mother of four, sister and a mother to many. Her name speaks to that calling—mother of many children—and her open heart has reached thousands of lives across Nigeria with care, prayer and the gospel.

A Nigerian from Benue State, she has dedicated her life to the ministry of Jesus Christ. She is a Christian and worker with the Redeemed Christian Church of God, Nigeria, serving women and families with humility, compassion and conviction.

Her life reflects the character of a virtuous woman: rooted in Christ, rich in wisdom and purposeful in service—an embodiment of the Proverbs 31 woman.

Enayi J. Ameh

What We Believe

A brief statement of the faith that undergirds everything we do.

✧

The Scriptures

We believe the Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God — our final rule for faith and living.

✧

The Godhead

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons — Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

✧

Salvation by Grace

We believe salvation is received by grace through faith in the finished work of Jesus Christ alone.

✧

A Transformed Life

We believe genuine faith expresses itself through love, character, service and obedience — not performance.

✧

The Church & Prayer

We believe in the local church, the priesthood of every believer, and prayer as the lifeline of a surrendered life.

Our Journey

A few chapters in a story still being written.

The Beginning

A Prayer Circle in Abuja

A small group of women began meeting to pray and study the Word — the seed of what would become The Unique Woman.

Early Years

First Outreach & First Devotional

The ministry stepped into its first community outreach while daily devotionals began circulating among the women in fellowship.

Growing

Conferences & Academic Support

Annual gatherings began drawing women from beyond Abuja, alongside the first scholarships for young women pursuing their education.

Today

A Movement, Still Growing

Mission support, humanitarian outreach, leadership development and daily discipleship — carried forward one obedient "yes" at a time.
